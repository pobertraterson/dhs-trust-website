Caring for your child

We know that your family is important to you, especially your infant. Getting the right calorie intake and other important health information can be difficult, so here's what you need to know.

Mount Vernon St, Liverpool L7 8YE

Important Information

If your child under 5 is having symptoms of an illness call 111. For anyone over 5 years old go online to 111 Online.

If your child has symptoms of severe breathing difficulties, serious injuries, seizures, allergic reactions, a heart attack, or a stroke, call 999 now.
