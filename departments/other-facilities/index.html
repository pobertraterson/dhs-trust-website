Other Facilities at the Royal Liverpool University Hospital

In the Royal Liverpool University Hospital, we have many facilities for patients and guests.


Catering

Skyline Restaurant

Located on the 10th floor, serves hot meals, drinks and sandwiches. Open weekdays 07:30 - 14:15.

Fresh

Located near main entrance, serves smoothies, wedges, and salads. Open weekdays 08:00 - 18:00.

Torino Italian Café

Located near main entrance, sells fresh pizza, ciabattas, coffee, and cake. Open weekdays 08:00 - 16:00.

The Bistro

Located on LG floor, serves coffee, hot and cold meals, and snacks. Open 24/7.

Linda McCartney Café

Located on ground floor, serves toasties, sandwiches, snacks, and drinks. Open weekdays, 08:00 - 15:00.


Shops and Other Facilities

WHSmith

Located on LG floor, sells Costa Coffee, magazines, books, health & beauty, drinks, and sandwiches Open 7 days per week 08:00 - 20:00.

Lloyds Pharmacy

Located near Linda McCartney Centre, open weekdays 08:30 - 17:00.

Amazon Lockers

Located near Skyline Restaurant.

Post box

Located on LG floor.
